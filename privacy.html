Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Processing

All tools on ToolboxKit run entirely in your browser. No text, data, or files you enter are ever sent to our servers or any third party. Your data stays on your device.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as Plausible or Umami) to understand which tools are most popular. These analytics do not track individuals and do not use cookies.

Cookies

ToolboxKit itself does not set cookies. Third-party ad providers may set cookies as described above.
