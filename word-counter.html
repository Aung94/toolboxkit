Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter work?

The word counter splits your text by whitespace to count words, counts all characters including spaces, detects sentences by punctuation marks (.!?), identifies paragraphs by blank lines, and estimates reading time at 200 words per minute.

What is the average reading speed?

The average adult reading speed is about 200-250 words per minute. This tool uses 200 WPM for a conservative estimate of reading time.

Does this tool count characters with and without spaces?

Yes. The tool shows both total characters (including spaces) and characters without spaces, which is useful for platforms that have character limits.
