Frequently Asked Questions

What voices are available?

The available voices depend on your operating system and browser. Most systems provide multiple English voices (male and female), and many also include voices for other languages.

Does this tool work offline?

Yes, once the page is loaded, the Text to Speech functionality uses your browser's built-in SpeechSynthesis API. No internet connection is needed for the actual speech synthesis.

Can I download the audio?

The browser's SpeechSynthesis API does not natively support downloading audio files. This tool plays the speech directly through your speakers.

Is there a character limit?

There is no hard limit in this tool, but some browsers may have internal limits on the SpeechSynthesis API (typically around 32,000 characters).
