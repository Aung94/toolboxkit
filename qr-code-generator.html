Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a QR code?

Simply enter your text or URL in the input field, choose your preferred size, and click Generate QR Code. The QR code is created instantly and can be downloaded as an image.

What can I encode in a QR code?

You can encode any text, URL, email address, phone number, WiFi credentials, or other information in a QR code. URLs are the most common use case.

Do QR codes expire?

Static QR codes (like those generated by this tool) never expire. The data is encoded directly in the QR code pattern. However, if the QR code points to a URL, the content at that URL could change or become unavailable.

What size should my QR code be?

For printing, 300x300 pixels or larger is recommended. For digital use, 200x200 is usually sufficient. The QR code should be large enough to be easily scanned by a smartphone camera.
