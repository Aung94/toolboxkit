Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Twitter character limit?

Twitter (X) allows up to 280 characters per tweet for standard accounts. URLs count as 23 characters regardless of length.

What is the Instagram caption character limit?

Instagram allows up to 2,200 characters for post captions. However, only the first 125 characters are shown before the "more" link.

What is the TikTok caption character limit?

TikTok allows up to 150 characters for video captions, including hashtags. Keep your captions concise and impactful.

What is the YouTube title character limit?

YouTube video titles can be up to 100 characters long. However, titles are truncated in search results at around 60-70 characters, so keep the most important keywords at the beginning.
